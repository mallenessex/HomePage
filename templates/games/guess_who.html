How It Works

Rules Snapshot

Each round, every active player secretly queues one action: Ask or Guess.

Questions all resolve together first, so every private board updates at the same time.

Queued guesses resolve after the question phase, so mutual knockouts and full-table chaos are possible.

At any time you can manually flip cards on your private board for that opponent.

A correct direct guess knocks out your target. A wrong direct guess knocks you out, just like the classic high-stakes finish.

Last active player standing wins. If everyone gets eliminated in the same resolution, the round ends in a draw.
